About Me

To intro ourseves to the group, please write 5 short phrases about yourself. These can be things you like, a mention of where you grew up, something you do or don't believe, a phobia, something no one else knows about you, etc. Be creative!

We are going to use these phrases to begin to explore HTML, and to build our first webpages together!

You can see 5 phrases I wrote about myself here. This page was written in HTML. HTML is used to identify and display content, not to style it, thats why it looks so boring. Here is what this page can look like with just a little bit of CSS applied.

Your final About Me page will have a photo, 5 short phrases about you, a link and should be styled with CSS.

To complete the project, post a link to your About Me page to our Google Community Event for "About Me."
